## Examples
> "They called off the wedding two weeks before the big day."

> 他們在大喜之日前兩週取消了婚禮。

## Usage tip
= cancel，**可拆**：call off the game／call the game off／代名詞一定塞中間 call it off（不可 ~~call off it~~）。也可指「叫…停手／撤回」：Call off your dog!（把你的狗叫回去！）

## Synonyms
cancel, scrap, abandon, call a halt to
