## Examples
> "As gossip spreads, people are quick to judge her."
> 隨著八卦傳開，大家急著評斷她。

> "Stop gossiping about your coworkers."
> 別再說同事的閒話了。

## Usage tip
Talk (often unkind) about other people's private lives — may be true or not.
- 名詞**不可數**：a piece of gossip / some juicy gossip（不是 a gossip 指八卦這件事；a gossip 在英式可指「愛講閒話的人」）。
- 動詞：gossip about sb。
- 辨析 vs **rumor**：gossip = 行為／閒聊內容（八卦）；rumor = 某個具體未證實的說法（謠言）。

## Synonyms
chitchat, hearsay, tittle-tattle (informal); 動詞 dish / spill the tea (slang)
