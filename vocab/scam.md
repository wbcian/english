## Examples
> "I almost thought the accommodation tax was a scam, but it's totally legit."

> 我差點以為宿泊税是詐騙，但它完全合法。

## Usage tip
超口語。正式文件用 `fraud`；日常幾乎都說 scam。  
常見搭配：`a scam artist` = 詐騙犯；`smells like a scam` = 感覺是在詐騙；`I got scammed` = 我被詐騙了。

## Synonyms
fraud (正式), con, hustle (俚語)
