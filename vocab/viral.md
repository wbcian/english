## Examples
> "The video went viral overnight."

> 那部影片一夜之間爆紅。

> "Tom Holland's Lip Sync Battle performance was massively viral, with nearly 200 million views."
> Tom Holland 在 Lip Sync Battle 的演出大爆紅，將近兩億觀看。

## Usage tip
- 動詞片語 **go viral** = 變成爆紅 (注意 "go" 是「變成」)
- 名詞用法 **viral moment** = 爆紅瞬間／片段
- **massively viral** = 大規模爆紅，副詞強調
