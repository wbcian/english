## Examples
> "I don't know how she puts up with his constant complaining."

> 我不懂她怎麼受得了他沒完沒了的抱怨。

## Usage tip
三字片語動詞 = tolerate。**不可拆**：受詞永遠在最後（put up with **it** ✅／~~put it up with~~ ❌）。語氣帶「不情願地忍」，受詞可是人或事。

## Synonyms
tolerate, stand, bear, endure, stomach
