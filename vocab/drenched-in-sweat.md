## Examples
> "After the five-kilometer run, I was completely drenched in sweat."

> 跑完五公里後，我全身濕透、滿是汗水。

## Usage tip
- drench (v.) ＝使濕透；be drenched in sweat ＝像整個人泡在汗裡。
- 結構：be + drenched（p.p. 當形容詞「濕透的」）+ in + sweat。
- 近義 soaked in sweat；動態誇張版 sweating buckets。

## Synonyms
soaked in sweat, sweating buckets, dripping wet
