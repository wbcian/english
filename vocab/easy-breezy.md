## Examples
> "We're just gonna keep it nice and easy breezy."

> 我們就保持輕鬆愉快就好。

> "Don't worry about the test, it'll be easy breezy."

> 別擔心考試，會很輕鬆的。

## Usage tip
- 來自名詞 **breeze**（微風）— 像微風吹過一樣輕鬆
- 名詞變體：**a breeze** = 一塊蛋糕／超簡單的事
  > "The exam was a breeze."
- 句型 **"nice and ___"** 是 native 口語常見模板：nice and warm, nice and quiet, nice and slow
- 注意：**taco peasy** 不是真片語，是搞笑押韻；正規版是 **easy peasy (lemon squeezy)**

## Synonyms
