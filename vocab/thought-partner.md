## Examples
> "He's been an amazing thought partner."

> 他一直是個超棒的**思考夥伴**。

> "I need a thought partner on this strategy doc — too many open questions."

> 這份策略文件我需要一個**思考夥伴**——太多沒解的問題。

> "Claude is increasingly being used as a thought partner, not just a code generator."

> Claude 越來越被當作**思考夥伴**，不只是 code generator。

## Usage tip
**`thought partner` ≠ advisor**——這是現代矽谷 / 顧問業流行詞。差別：

| 詞 | 關係 | 意思 |
|---|---|---|
| advisor | 不對等 | 上對下「給建議」 |
| mentor | 不對等 | 上對下「指導」 |
| **thought partner** | **對等** | 互相 brainstorm、挑戰想法 |
| collaborator | 對等 | 一起做事（不只是思考） |

最常用搭配：
- `be a great / amazing thought partner`
- `I need a thought partner on [N]`
- `they served as my thought partner`

⚠️ 中文沒有完美對應——「思考夥伴」最接近，「軍師」太古、「智囊」太重。

### Cian 套用
描述同事時：`She's been an incredible thought partner on this redesign.` 比 `She helped a lot` 強 3 倍。
