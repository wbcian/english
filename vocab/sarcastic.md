## Examples
> "Oh great, another Monday. — Wow, don't be so sarcastic."
> 「喔太棒了，又是星期一。」——「哇，別這麼酸。」

> "She gave a sarcastic smile when he showed up two hours late."
> 他遲到兩小時才出現，她露出一抹諷刺的笑。

## Usage tip
Saying the **opposite of what you mean**, with a mocking/cutting tone, to tease or criticize. Tone carries the meaning.
- Noun **sarcasm** /ˈsɑːrkæzəm/ 諷刺；adverb **sarcastically** 諷刺地.
- Collocations: **dripping with sarcasm**, "a hint of sarcasm".
- 🇹🇼 matches everyday 「酸」/「講反話」.
- ⚠️ Tone-dependent — doesn't survive flat text well (hence 🙄/😏 and "/s" online). Big in British & American humor.
- Stress on middle: sar-CAS-tic.

## Synonyms
mocking, snide, cutting, dry (humor), tongue-in-cheek (more playful)
