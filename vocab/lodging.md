## Examples
> "The accommodation tax applies to all paid lodging in Tokyo."

> 住宿税適用於東京所有收費住宿。

## Usage tip
比 `accommodation`（英國腔）更偏美式、正式，常見於法律/稅務/政府文件。  
日常說話更常用 `a place to stay`、`somewhere to sleep`、`my Airbnb`。看到 lodging 基本上是書面語。  
`board and lodging` = 食宿（含餐）；`room and board` 同義。

## Synonyms
accommodation, quarters, board and lodging
