## Examples
> "I ate my shaved ice too fast and got an instant brain freeze."

> 我刨冰吃太快，馬上就一陣大腦凍結（頭部刺痛）。

## Usage tip
- 口語，指吃冰太快造成的短暫劇烈頭痛。搭配 get／have a brain freeze。
- 也可比喻「腦袋一片空白、當機」：I had a total brain freeze in the interview.
- 正式醫學名 ice cream headache。

## Synonyms
ice cream headache, freeze headache
