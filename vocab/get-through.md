## Examples

> "Everyone knows you're going to get us through it."
> 大家都知道你會帶我們撐過去。

> "We'll get through it, sweetie. Breathe. Deep breath."
> 我們會撐過去的，深呼吸。

> "I couldn't get through to him on the phone."
> 我電話打不通給他。

> "The message finally got through."
> 訊息終於傳達到了。

## Usage tip

主要兩個常見意思，靠介係詞區分：

1. **get through (sth)** = 撐過某段難熬時期／完成某件吃力的事。
   - `I just need to get through this week.` 我只要撐過這禮拜就好。
   - `She got through three books on the flight.` 她在飛機上嗑掉三本書。

2. **get through to (sb)** = 聯絡到某人（電話）／讓某人聽進去。
   - `I tried calling but couldn't get through.`
   - `Nothing I say gets through to him.` 我說什麼他都聽不進去。

關鍵對比：
| | go through | get through |
|---|---|---|
| 重點 | 正在經歷（中間） | 成功熬到結束（出口） |
| 例句 | We're going through it. | We'll get through it. |

寫作時兩個常配對：先講 going through，再用 get through 給希望。
