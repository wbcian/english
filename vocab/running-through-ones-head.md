## Examples
> "That was just not running through my head when I made *Espresso*."
> 寫《Espresso》的時候我腦子裡根本沒有那回事。

> "Espresso is probably running through your head right now."
> 你現在腦中應該正在循環《Espresso》吧。

> "That song's been running through my head all day."
> 那首歌在我腦中循環了一整天。

## Usage tip
- 主詞通常是 **想法 / 旋律 / 回憶 / 句子**（重複性的東西）。
- 跟 "stuck in my head"（耳蟲）幾乎同義，但 running through 更廣——可指任何揮之不去的念頭，不只歌。
- 對比：`cross one's mind`（一閃而過）VS `run through one's head`（重複播放）。

## Synonyms
stuck in my head, on my mind, can't stop thinking about, on repeat
