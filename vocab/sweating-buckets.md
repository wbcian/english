## Examples
> "The AC broke, and within minutes we were all sweating buckets."

> 冷氣壞了，沒幾分鐘我們全都汗如雨下。

## Usage tip
- 誇飾 idiom：汗多到「一桶一桶」（buckets）。結構 be sweating buckets。
- 比 "I'm very sweaty" 生動得多；口語抱怨流汗首選。
- 近義 be drenched in sweat（整個人濕透）。

## Synonyms
drenched in sweat, dripping with sweat, soaked
