## Examples
> "Before quitting his job, he tested the waters by freelancing on weekends."
> 在辭職前，他先用週末接案來試試水溫。

> "We're launching in one city first to test the waters."
> 我們先在一個城市上市，試試市場反應。

## Usage tip
Image = dipping a toe in before jumping in: a small, low-risk trial to gauge a reaction/situation **before fully committing**.
- Idiom default is **plural "waters"** (literal "test the water" = check actual water, different meaning).
- Big in business/career contexts (market, idea, job change, sounding people out).
- Common patterns: "test the waters **by** V-ing", "...**before** committing".
- 🇹🇼 maps ~1:1 onto 「試水溫」.

## Synonyms
dip a toe in, feel it out, gauge the reaction, run it up the flagpole (casual)
