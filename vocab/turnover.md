## Examples
> "Over the last year, turnover was really high, and the product team ended up with a real gap."

> 過去一年人員流動率很高，產品團隊因此出現了一個很大的人力缺口。

> "The company's annual turnover reached $2 million."

> 這家公司的年營業額達到兩百萬美元。

## Usage tip
多義字，靠語境分辨：
- **HR 語境**（員工來來去去）＝ **員工流動率／離職率**：`turnover was high`、`low turnover rate`、`staff turnover`
- **財務語境**（尤其英式英文）＝ **營業額**：`annual turnover of $2M`（美式較常說 revenue）

## Synonyms
（HR 義）attrition, churn；（財務義）revenue, sales volume
