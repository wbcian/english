## Examples
> "Talking to his plants is just one of his quirks."
> 跟植物講話只是他眾多怪癖之一。

> "By a strange quirk of fate, they ended up on the same flight."
> 命運的奇妙安排下，他們最後搭上了同一班飛機。

## Usage tip
A small, odd, often **endearing** habit or feature — usually charming/harmless, not a serious flaw.
- **quirk of fate / nature** = 命運／自然的巧妙安排（固定搭配）
- Adjective **quirky** /ˈkwɜːrki/ = 古怪又有趣的（帶褒義）— "a quirky café", "a quirky sense of humor"
- Nuance: quirk (charming/neutral) vs. flaw (真缺陷) vs. weird (偏負面). 說人 quirky 基本上是稱讚。

## Synonyms
idiosyncrasy, peculiarity, eccentricity, oddity
