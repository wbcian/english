## Examples
> "It means to relax, not work too hard, or not do anything too intensive."
> 意思是放輕鬆，不要太拼，不要做太密集／太重的事情。

> "I'm taking an intensive English course this summer."
> 我這個夏天要上密集英文課。

## Usage tip
- ⚠️ 別跟 **intense** 搞混：
  - **intense** /ɪnˈtens/ → 強烈、激烈（多形容感受、情緒）
    > "intense pain" / "intense gaze"
  - **intensive** /ɪnˈtensɪv/ → 密集、強化（多形容方法、訓練、勞動）
    > "intensive training" / "intensive care unit (ICU)"
- 常見搭配：intensive course, intensive care, labor-intensive, capital-intensive

## Synonyms
concentrated, rigorous, thorough, in-depth
