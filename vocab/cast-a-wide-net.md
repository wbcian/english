## Examples

> "Be intentional and cast a wide net — find 4 people who have done that thing successfully."

> 要刻意地廣撒網——找 4 位真的把這件事做成過的人。

> "I find these conversations less good for solving specific problems, and better for casting the net wide and seeing what you find."

> 我發現這類對話不太適合解決具體問題，但很適合廣撒網看看會撈到什麼。

## Usage tip

- 漁網比喻：把網撒寬，撈到的可能性才大
- 商業／求職／找對象都常用：`cast a wide net when job hunting`
- 變體：`cast the net wide` 也 ok
- 反向：`narrow the search`（縮小範圍）

## Synonyms / Related

broad search, explore widely, keep options open, leave no stone unturned
