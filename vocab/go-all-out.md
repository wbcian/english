## Examples
> "Tom surprised everyone by going all out with fishnets, a wig, and incredible choreography."
> Tom 卯足全力穿漁網襪、戴假髮、配上瘋狂編舞，讓所有人嚇一跳。

> "We went all out for her birthday party."
> 我們為她的生日派對火力全開。

## Usage tip
- 常見句型：**go all out for [event]** / **go all out on [thing]**
- 帶有「不保留任何餘力、玩很大」的情緒色彩
- 與 "take it easy"（放輕鬆）正好相反

## Synonyms
give it your all, pull out all the stops, leave nothing on the table
