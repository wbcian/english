## Usage tip

emotion 強度量表（弱 → 強）：
**afraid / scared < frightened < terrified < petrified / horrified**

`terrified` 已經是「嚇到動彈不得」級別，前面**不要加 very**——native ear 聽起來很怪。要加副詞用 *absolutely / completely / utterly terrified*。

句型：
- `be terrified of [N / V-ing]` — 害怕某事物。`terrified of flying`
- `be terrified to [V]` — 怕到不敢做某事。`terrified to ask`
- `be terrified that [子句]` — 害怕…會發生。`terrified that I'll fail`

來自動詞 **terrify**（使極度害怕）。被動形容詞化：terrify (v.) → terrified (被嚇到的) / terrifying (令人害怕的)。

⚠️ 別搞混：
- **terrified** = 我害怕（主詞是感受方）
- **terrifying** = 令人害怕的（主詞是來源）
- `The horror movie was terrifying. I was terrified.`
