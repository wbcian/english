## Examples
> "Let's push back the meeting to Friday — half the team is out sick."

> 我們把會議延到星期五吧——團隊一半的人請病假了。

## Usage tip
作「延後」時 = postpone，**可拆**：push back the meeting／push the meeting back／受詞是代名詞時 push it back（不可 ~~push back it~~）。另一個高頻義是名詞 **pushback**＝反對、抵制（We got a lot of pushback on the new policy.）。

## Synonyms
postpone, delay, put off, reschedule
