## Examples
> "After his internship ended, the company offered him a full-time position with full benefits."

> 實習結束後，公司提供他一個附完整福利的全職職位。

> "Joe got offered a full-time position as a teacher. Theoretically, it's a great opportunity — a stable salary, benefits. But it's not his dream."

> Joe 被提供了一個全職教師的職位。理論上這是個很棒的機會——穩定的薪水、福利。但那不是他的夢想。

## Usage tip
對比 `part-time position`（兼職職位）。常見搭配動詞：`offer/get/accept/turn down a full-time position`。
出自 Pixar《Soul》：Ethan 用這個詞點出 Joe 的兩難——全職教職代表安全感（security），但代價是放棄追逐爵士樂夢想的機會。
延伸：`security`（保障感，此處指工作／財務上的穩定）常與 full-time position 一起出現。

## Synonyms
permanent position, full-time job, permanent role
