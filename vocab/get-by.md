## Examples
> "My Japanese isn't great, but I can get by when I travel."

> 我的日文不怎麼樣，但旅行時還能勉強應付。

## Usage tip
指「資源不多但勉強過關」——錢、語言、能力都可用。常接 **on**（靠多少錢過活：get by on $1,000 a month）或 **with/without**（get by with a little help）。課堂口語把 "get by **by** using…" 吞成 "get by using…"。

## Synonyms
manage, cope, make do, scrape by, survive
