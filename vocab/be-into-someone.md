## Examples
> "Dude, you are so into her. You've checked your phone like fifty times waiting for her text."

> 老兄，你超迷她的，你等她訊息已經看手機大概五十次了。

> "He's really into you — he doesn't act like that with anyone else."

> 他真的很喜歡你，他對別人不會這樣。

## Usage tip
跟 [[be-interested-in-someone]] 的溫度差：`interested in` 含蓄、剛萌芽；`into` 已經**上頭、栽進去了**，口語、朋友間閒聊常用，帶點藏不住的意味。
`into` 也能用在興趣上、跟感情無關：`I'm into rock climbing`（我很迷攀岩）。
記憶鉤：into = 陷「進去」了。

## Synonyms
be smitten with someone, have a crush on someone, be crazy about someone
