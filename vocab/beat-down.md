## Examples
> "The sun was beating down on the beach, and there was no shade in sight."

> 烈日無情地曝曬著海灘，一點遮蔭也沒有。

## Usage tip
- beat down (on sb/sth) ＝陽光「直直打下來、猛烈曝曬」的擬人化畫面。
- the sun is beating down 比 "it's very sunny" 生動太多，寫作／口語都加分。
- beat down 另有「打倒、殺價」等義，靠上下文分辨。

## Synonyms
blaze down, bear down
