## Examples
> "He spreads rumors telling a different version of what happened."
> 他散播謠言，講了一個不同版本的經過。

> "There's a rumor that the company is being sold."
> 有謠言說公司要被賣掉。

## Usage tip
Information passed person to person that may or may not be true.
- 英式拼 **rumour**，美式 **rumor**。
- 高頻搭配：**spread / start / deny / squash** a rumor；rumor has it (that)…
- 被動句很常見：It is rumored that…（據傳…）。
- 辨析 vs [[gossip]]：rumor 是一個具體未證實的「說法」；gossip 是「閒聊八卦」這個行為／內容。

## Synonyms
hearsay, buzz, word on the street, [[gossip]]
