## Examples
> "You're walking around every single cafe."

> 你每一間咖啡廳都繞遍了。

> "I've watched every single episode of Seinfeld at least three times."

> 《歡樂單身派對》每一集我都至少看過三次。

> "She meditates every single day."

> 她每一天都打坐冥想，一天都沒漏。

## Usage tip
- `every` 已經有「每個」的意思，加上 `single` 是為了強調**零例外**、戲劇感。
- 文法上 `every single + 單數可數名詞`：every single day / time / episode / person。
- 常搭情緒副詞：`every single damn time`（每次都他媽的這樣）—— 加 damn / fucking 火力全開，注意場合。

## Synonyms
each and every, every last one, without exception
