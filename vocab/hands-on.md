## Examples
> "I learn best with a hands-on approach — just let me try it."
> 我用實作的方式學最快——直接讓我上手試。

> "She's a hands-on manager who works alongside her team."
> 她是個事必躬親、跟團隊並肩做事的主管。

## Usage tip
Directly involved by **doing**, not just observing/theorizing.
- Hyphenated before a noun: **a hands-on workshop**, **hands-on experience** 實務經驗.
- Opposite = **hands-off** 放手不管的.
- Big in work/learning contexts; TOEIC-friendly.

## Synonyms
practical, experiential, interactive, involved
