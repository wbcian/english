## Examples
> "Here's your itemized bill — please take a look and confirm everything is correct."

> 這是您的明細帳單，請看一下、確認是否都正確。

## Usage tip
`itemize` = 逐項列出（動詞）；英式拼法 `itemised`。
退房核帳、報帳、報銷（expense report）都很常用。對比籠統的 `total`。

## Synonyms
detailed bill, breakdown, statement
