## Examples
> "The subway was sweltering, and everyone was fanning themselves."

> 地鐵裡又悶又熱，每個人都在搧風。

## Usage tip
- swelter (v.) ＝熱得發昏；sweltering＝形容詞「悶熱難受的」。
- 關鍵差別：**scorching＝很熱**，**sweltering＝很熱＋悶到不舒服**。想強調「難受」就用 sweltering。
- 例：Europe suffers under sweltering heat.（歐洲飽受酷暑煎熬。）

## Synonyms
stifling, oppressive, sultry, muggy
