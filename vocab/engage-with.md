## Examples
> "You take action yourself to engage with it, and learn as you engage with it."

> 你親自採取行動去投入它，並且邊投入邊學。

> "Students learn more when they actively engage with the material."

> 學生主動投入教材時學得更多。

## Usage tip
- 比 use / read 更「主動、深入」：engage with 強調你有**真的投入心力去互動**，不是被動接收。
- 高頻搭配：engage with **the material / the content / customers / the community / an idea**。
- 也可不接 with 當及物：engage the audience（吸引住觀眾）。但「投入某內容」這個意思時，介系詞 with 不能省。

## Synonyms
interact with, dive into, get involved with, immerse oneself in
