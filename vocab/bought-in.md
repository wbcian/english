## Examples
> "Making sure our marketing team, sales team, finance capacity, etc., are bought in on the plan."

> 確保 marketing、sales、finance 都對這計畫**買單**了。

> "I'm not fully bought in yet — can you walk me through the data?"

> 我還沒完全**買單**——可以走一遍數據給我看嗎？

> "Once the CEO bought in, the rest of the org fell in line quickly."

> CEO 一**買單**，整個 org 就跟上了。

## Usage tip
**「買進」這個動作 → 「精神上認同並投入」**。商業圈、startup、政治都常用。

兩種形式：
- `bought in` 形容詞用法（已經買單的狀態）→ `they're bought in`
- `buy in (to N)` 動詞片語 → `you need to buy in to the vision`

衍生名詞：
- `buy-in` (n.) → `we need buy-in from the engineering team`（我們需要工程團隊的認同）

⚠️ 不是字面「買東西」——是「**心理上同意且承諾**」。

### Cian 工作場合
- `Engineering is fully bought in on the migration.` 工程團隊完全認同這次遷移。
- `We don't have buy-in from leadership yet.` 我們還沒拿到管理層的認同。
- `Before we ship, get buy-in from legal.` 出貨前，先拿到 legal 的同意。

對比中文「同意」——`buy-in` 比 agreement 更**主動、更承諾投入**。「同意」只是不反對，buy-in 是「願意為這件事努力」。
