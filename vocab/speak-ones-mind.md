## Examples
> "Anne isn't afraid to speak her mind, even when it gets her into trouble."
> Anne 不怕直言不諱，就算會給她惹麻煩也一樣。

> "I appreciate people who speak their minds in meetings."
> 我欣賞那種在會議上敢說真話的人。

## Usage tip
To say what you really think, honestly and directly.
- Possessive must agree: speak **your / her / his / their** mind.
- Often + "freely" / "even if…": speak your mind freely.
- 中性偏褒（敢說、坦率），但場合不對也可能顯得太衝。

## Synonyms
be outspoken, tell it like it is, say what you think, be candid
