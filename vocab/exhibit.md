## ⚠️ Pronunciation
**h is SILENT**: eg-ZIB-it /ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/, NOT "ex-HIB-it". Stress on the middle.
(But the noun **exhibition** /ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃən/ DOES sound the h: ek-suh-BISH-un.)

## Examples
### ① (v.) display publicly — art, products
> "The gallery is exhibiting local artists this month."
> 這間畫廊這個月展出在地藝術家的作品。

### ② (v.) show a quality/behavior (formal)
> "The patient exhibited symptoms of the flu."
> 病人出現了流感的症狀。

### ③ (n.) display item / legal evidence
> "My favorite exhibit at the museum was the dinosaur skeleton."
> 我最喜歡博物館裡那具恐龍骨架的展品。

> "Exhibit A" = （法庭）證物甲。

## Usage tip
- Noun **exhibition** = 展覽（whole event）vs. **exhibit** (n.) = a single item, or AmE for the show itself. TOEIC: **trade exhibition / exhibition hall**.
- Sense ② ("exhibit symptoms/signs/behavior") is formal — casual speech uses **show**.

## Synonyms
① display, showcase, present　② show, demonstrate, display
