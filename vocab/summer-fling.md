## Examples
> "They had a summer fling in Miami, but it fizzled out by September."

> 他們在邁阿密有過一段夏日戀情，但九月就無疾而終了。

## Usage tip
- fling（n.）本義「短暫、不認真地投入一下」；summer fling ＝一段來去匆匆的夏天戀愛。
- 比 summer romance 更強調「短暫、玩玩、不長久」。動詞用 have a summer fling。

## Synonyms
summer romance, short-lived romance, holiday romance
