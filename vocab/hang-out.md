## Examples
> "We usually just hang out at his place and play video games."

> 我們通常就在他家閒晃、打電動。

## Usage tip
母語人士超高頻字＝spend time（with sb / doing sth）。常接 **with**（hang out with friends）或地點（hang out at the mall）。名詞 **hangout**＝常去的地方。不可拆。

## Synonyms
spend time, chill, kick back, socialize
