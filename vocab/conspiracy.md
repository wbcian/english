## Examples
> "There's no evidence for the conspiracy theory."

> "They were charged with conspiracy to commit fraud."
> 他們被控共謀詐欺。

## Usage tip
A secret plan by a group to do something harmful/illegal. Most common form = **conspiracy theory** 陰謀論 (~95% of real usage).
- ⚠️ Stress trap: con-**SPIR**-uh-see /kənˈspɪrəsi/ — stress 2nd syllable; the o's are quiet schwas.
- Verb **conspire** /kənˈspaɪər/ 共謀 ("they conspired to...").
- Adjective conspiratorial (low priority).

## Synonyms
plot, scheme, collusion, cover-up
