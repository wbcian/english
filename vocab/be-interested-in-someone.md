## Examples
> "Ever since the meeting, he keeps stealing glances at her across the room — pretty obvious he's interested in her."

> 自從那場會議之後，他就一直隔著房間偷瞄她——很明顯他對她有意思。

> "I think she's interested in you. She laughed at literally all your bad jokes."

> 我覺得她對你有意思，你那些爛笑話她全都笑了。

## Usage tip
最安全、最中性的「有好感」說法，曖昧初期、還沒表白時最好用。介系詞固定 `in`，不是 ❌ *interested to someone*。
偷看／偷瞄的道地講法：`steal glances at someone`、`sneak a peek at someone`。
溫度光譜：`interested in`（含蓄、剛開始）＜ [[be-into-someone]]（已經很喜歡）＜ `have a crush on`（迷戀、較青澀）。
