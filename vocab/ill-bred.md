## Examples
> "Besides, Josie's always been ill-bred."
> 再說，Josie 本來就沒家教。

> "Interrupting an elder like that is terribly ill-bred."
> 那樣打斷長輩說話，實在很沒教養。

## Usage tip
An insult: someone wasn't taught to behave properly.
- 拆字：**ill**（差、不好）＋ **bred**（breed 的過去分詞，原指「養動物」）→ 字面「養得很差／沒教好」。
- 反義 **well-bred**（有教養的）。
- ⚠️ **Register trap**：老派、文學／影劇味，帶強烈評判。看《Anne With An E》這種古裝劇要懂，但現代日常別拿來罵人。想表達同意思，口語多用 rude / has no manners / badly raised。

## Synonyms
rude, uncouth, badly brought up, boorish；反義 well-bred, well-mannered
