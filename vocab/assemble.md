## Examples
### ① 組裝、拼裝（put parts together）
> "It took me two hours to assemble the IKEA shelf."
> 我花了兩小時才組裝好那個 IKEA 層架。

### ② 集合、聚集（gather people/things）
> "The students assembled in the hall for the announcement."
> 學生們在禮堂集合聽宣布事項。

## Usage tip
- Noun **assembly** /əˈsɛmbli/ = 組裝；集會；議會. TOEIC collocations: **assembly line** 生產線, **assembly instructions** 組裝說明.
- The "gather" sense is slightly **formal** — casual speech prefers get together / gather / round up. "Assemble the team" sounds official/heroic.
- Regular verb: assemble / assembled / assembled.
- Stress on the middle: a-SSEM-ble; first e = schwa.

## Synonyms
- ① build, put together, construct
- ② gather, convene, round up
