## Examples
> "Don't be a smart aleck — just answer the question."
> 別在那耍嘴皮子，好好回答問題。

> "Some smart aleck in the back kept correcting the teacher."
> 後排有個自作聰明的傢伙一直糾正老師。

## Usage tip
A person who's annoyingly clever — cheeky, sarcastic, know-it-all remarks to show off. Usually light/teasing, not deeply insulting.
- Adjective: **smart-alecky** = 愛耍嘴皮的（"a smart-alecky comment"）.
- Noun-for-a-person (or adj.), not a verb.
- Also spelled "smart alec".

## ⚠️ Register / 實用性 note
Understood by everyone but sounds slightly **dated / parents'-generation**. More natural modern swaps:
- **wise guy** — common, "OK, wise guy" (context can also = mafia)
- **smart-ass** ⚠️ vulgar — most common in casual adult speech, but swear-ish
- **know-it-all** — if emphasis is "thinks they know everything"
Verdict: **passive keeper** (recognize in older movies/sitcoms); for active use prefer wise guy / smart-ass.

## Synonyms
wise guy, smart-ass (vulgar), know-it-all, wiseacre
