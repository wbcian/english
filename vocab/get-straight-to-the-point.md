## Examples
> "This lesson is so popular because it gets straight to the point."

> 這堂課這麼受歡迎，就是因為它直接切入重點。

## Usage tip
= get to the point（不繞圈子）。`straight` 加強「直接、毫不拖泥帶水」。反義是 **beat around the bush**（旁敲側擊、講話繞圈）。催人講重點：Just get to the point!（你就直說吧！）

## Synonyms
get to the point, cut to the chase, be direct
