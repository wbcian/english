## Examples
> "Could I get a wake-up call at 6:30 tomorrow morning?"

> 明天早上六點半可以幫我設個叫醒服務嗎？

## Usage tip
⚠️ **morning call 是和製英語（Japanese-English）**——英語母語者一律說 **wake-up call**。
動詞搭 `set / request / get a wake-up call`。
比喻義：a **wake-up call** = 一記警鐘、讓人醒悟的事件（"The diagnosis was a wake-up call."）。

## Synonyms
alarm call, morning call (✗ non-native)
