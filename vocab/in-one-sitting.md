## Examples
> "You don't have to learn a bunch of new phrasal verbs in one sitting."

> 你不必一次坐下來就硬塞一大堆新的片語動詞。

## Usage tip
強調「不間斷、一次完成」，常配 read／watch／finish／eat（read the whole book in one sitting 一口氣把整本書看完）。也說 **at one sitting**。對比本課訊息：與其 in one sitting，不如每天累積讓它 stack up。

## Synonyms
all at once, in one go, at a stretch
