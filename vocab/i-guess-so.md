## Examples
> "Was the movie good?" — "I guess so."

> 「電影好看嗎？」「大概吧。」

> "So I guess this means we're over." — "Yeah, I guess so."

> 「所以我們算是結束了？」「嗯，大概吧。」

## Usage tip
= probably / I suppose。不確定或興趣缺缺時用，**語氣冷淡保留**，不是熱情的同意。想表達肯定別用它。

## Synonyms
I suppose so, probably, kind of
