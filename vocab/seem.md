## Examples
> "You seem tired today — did you not sleep well?"

> 你今天看起來很累——是不是沒睡好？

> "It seems (that) they forgot about the meeting entirely."

> 他們好像完全忘了有這場會議。

## Usage tip
連綴動詞（linking verb），後面直接接**形容詞**（seem happy／tired／odd），不是副詞。三個高頻句型：① **seem + adj.**（You seem nervous）；② **seem to + V**（She seems to know everyone）；③ **It seems (that) + 子句**。禮貌軟化神器——"It seems there's a mistake" 比 "There is a mistake" 委婉很多，職場／客服好用。
