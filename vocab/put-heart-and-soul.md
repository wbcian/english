## Examples
> "She put her heart and soul into the project."
> 她全心全意投入這個專案。

> "He put his heart and soul into raising his kids."
> 他全心全意地養育他的孩子。

> "If you put your heart and soul into it, you'll succeed."
> 只要你全心投入，就會成功。

## Usage tip

**句型：** `put + one's + heart and soul + into + N/V-ing`
- one's 要用所有格（my / your / his / her / their）
- into 後面接名詞或動名詞 -ing

⚠️ Cian 常打成 "hear and soul" — **是 heart ❤️**（多一個 t）。

**用法核心：** 強調「不只時間，連靈魂、情感都投入」— 比 `work hard` 強烈很多。

## 親兄弟 idioms（意思幾乎相同）

| Idiom | 語感差異 |
|---|---|
| **put one's heart and soul into** | 強調**情感、靈魂**投入 |
| **pour one's blood, sweat, and tears into** | 強調**辛苦勞動**、過程艱辛 |
| **give it your all** | 最口語、最簡短 |
| **throw oneself into** | 強調全身心、不顧一切 |
| **go all out** | 動作層面、火力全開 |

## Synonyms
devote oneself to, throw oneself into, give your all, pour everything into
