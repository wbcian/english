## Examples
> "Would it be possible to have a late check-out?"

> 有可能延後退房嗎？

## Usage tip
名詞 `check-out` 有連字號；常需加費（`for an additional fee`）且看房況。
請求句固定搭 `Would it be possible to have a late check-out?`。對義詞 `early check-in`。

## Synonyms
later checkout, extended checkout
