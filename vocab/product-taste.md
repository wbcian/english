## Examples
> "We're pretty focused on hiring engineers with great product taste."
> 我們很專注在找有產品品味的工程師。

> "As code becomes cheaper to write, the thing that becomes more valuable is product taste — deciding what to write."
> 程式碼變便宜後，更值錢的是 product taste——決定要寫什麼。

## Usage tip
矽谷／AI 圈高頻詞。中文沒有完美對應——硬翻「產品品味」聽起來怪，但**它指的是「3 秒內判斷一個 feature 會不會 work」的直覺**。

- 描述人：`She has great product taste.`
- 描述決策依據：`This comes down to product taste.`
- 補強自己論點：`From a product-taste perspective, ...`

不要用 `product sense` 替換——意思接近但 `taste` 更強調**美感+判斷力**的綜合直覺。

## Synonyms
product sense, product intuition, product judgment
