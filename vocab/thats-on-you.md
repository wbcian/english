## Examples
> "That's on you because now you can't go buy espressos anywhere without somebody going, 'That's that me espresso.'"
> 這你自找的，現在去哪買 espresso 都會有人對你唱「That's that me espresso」。

> "What if somebody had died tonight? Different story, right? 'Cause that's on you."

> 萬一今晚有人死了呢？那就是另一回事了，因為那就是你的責任。

> "You picked the restaurant — if it sucks, that's on you."

> 餐廳你挑的，難吃算你帳上。

## Usage tip
- 結構：`that's on + 人`。最常見三種：`that's on you / me / them`。
- 場景：（1）推鍋指責，（2）認錯擔責，（3）開玩笑互嗆。
- 變體：
  - `that's on me` = 我來扛 / 算我的（也常用在「我請客」的場合）
  - `that's all on you` = 全是你的責任（語氣更重）
- 跟 `it's your fault` 比起來，**that's on you 比較中性**——指向責任歸屬，未必是嚴厲指控。

## Synonyms
that's your fault, that's your problem, that's your responsibility, you brought this on yourself
