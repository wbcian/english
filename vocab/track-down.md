## Examples
> "It took me three weeks to track down the original author of that quote."
> 我花了三個禮拜才追查到那句名言的原作者。

> "We're still trying to track down the cause of the outage."
> 我們還在追查停機的原因。

## Usage tip
Separable phrasal verb — object can sit in the middle ("track him down", "track down the file"). With a pronoun (him/her/it/them) it **must** split: ✅ "track it down" / ❌ "track down it".
Common objects: a person, a source, a document, a phone number, the cause/origin of something.
Always implies deliberate effort — you don't *accidentally* track something down (contrast with plain "find").

## Synonyms
hunt down, locate, dig up, trace
