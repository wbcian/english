## Examples
> "An 'Espresso' sign at a cafe has nothing to do with me."
> 咖啡廳的 espresso 招牌跟我一點關係都沒有。

> "It has nothing to do with you personally — I find you extraordinarily professional."
> 這完全不是針對你個人——我覺得你超專業的。

## Usage tip
切割關係 / 撇清誤會時的萬用句。三種變化都很常用：
- `has nothing to do with me/you/this`
- `had nothing to do with ~`（過去式）
- `something to do with` ↔ 反義：跟…有關

連音時 "to" 弱化 + tap T → [nothing-duh-do with me]，跟讀練習對節奏超有幫助。

## Synonyms
unrelated to, irrelevant to, not connected to
