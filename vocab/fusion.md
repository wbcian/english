## Examples
> "This restaurant serves Korean-Mexican fusion."
> 這家餐廳賣韓墨融合料理。

> "The band's sound is a fusion of jazz and hip-hop."
> 這個樂團的音樂是爵士與嘻哈的融合。

> "Nuclear fusion powers the sun."
> 核融合是太陽的能量來源。

## Usage tip
Merging two+ distinct things into one unified whole.
- Everyday: **fusion cuisine** 融合料理, **fusion (music)** 融合曲風, **a fusion of [styles/ideas/cultures]**.
- Science: **nuclear fusion** 核融合（合併原子，太陽/氫彈）⚠️ 對比 **fission** 核分裂（分裂原子，核電廠）— opposite processes!
- Verb **fuse** /fjuːz/ 融合、熔接（"the companies fused into one"）; note noun *fuse* = 保險絲/引信（不同日常義）.
- Pronunciation: middle = soft /ʒ/ (as in "measure").
