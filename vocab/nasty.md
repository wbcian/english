## Examples
> "How dare you spread nasty rumors about my friend!"
> 你怎麼敢散播關於我朋友的惡毒謠言！

> "It left a nasty taste in my mouth. / We're in for some nasty weather."
> 它在我嘴裡留下一股難聞的味道。／我們要碰上一場糟糕的天氣了。

## Usage tip
Very versatile "unpleasant / bad" word — tone comes from the noun it modifies:
- **人 / 話語**：mean, unkind — a nasty comment, a nasty person.
- **天氣 / 情況**：bad, severe — nasty weather, a nasty surprise.
- **味道 / 傷口**：unpleasant, serious — a nasty smell, a nasty cut.
- 口語也可當「糟糕／嚴重」泛用。比較 mild 的講法可換 unpleasant / mean。

## Synonyms
unpleasant, mean, vicious, foul, horrible
