## Examples
> "We were going to go to the beach, but ended up going to the movie theater instead."

> 我們本來要去海邊，結果最後跑去了電影院。

## Usage tip
談「最終結果」，後面常接 **V-ing**（end up doing）、**形容詞**（end up broke）或**地點**（end up in jail）。帶一點「計畫之外、陰錯陽差」的味道。不可拆。

## Synonyms
turn out, result in, wind up
