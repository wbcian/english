## Examples
> "My roommate pulled a prank on me by hiding my alarm clock — I nearly slept through my final exam."

> 我室友對我惡作劇，把我的鬧鐘藏起來——我差點睡過頭錯過期末考。

> "They pranked their teacher on April Fools' Day by covering her desk in sticky notes."

> 他們在愚人節惡作劇老師，把她的桌子貼滿便利貼。

## Usage tip
招牌搭配是名詞用法 **pull a prank (on someone)**，動詞固定用 "pull" 不是 "do" 或 "make"。當及物動詞用時直接 "prank someone"，語感偏輕鬆／惡意較低。

## Synonyms
practical joke（較正式）、trick、stunt
Related: April Fools' Day、prankster（惡作劇的人）
