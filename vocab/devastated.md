## Examples
> "I have eyes, and I can see that she is devastated."
> 我有眼睛，我看得出來她整個人都崩潰了。

> "She was devastated when he broke up with her last summer."
> 去年夏天他跟她分手時，她整個人崩潰了。

## Usage tip
Completely destroyed emotionally — far stronger than "sad".
- 句型：be devastated **by** sth／be devastated **when**…／**to hear / find out** that…
- 份量很重：分手、落榜、親人或寵物過世才用，別拿來形容「有點失望」。
- 另一義（本義）：devastate = 摧毀（土地／城市），devastated area = 受災地區。

## Synonyms
crushed, heartbroken, shattered, gutted (British informal)
