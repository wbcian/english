## Examples
> "I suddenly want a strong cup of Joe."
> 我突然好想來杯濃咖啡。

> "I can't function without my morning cup of Joe."
> 沒喝早上那杯咖啡我整個無法運作。

## Usage tip
Joe = coffee。美式口語，比 "a cup of coffee" 更隨性道地。點餐、聊天都好用。

## Synonyms
a cup of coffee, a brew, a cuppa（英式偏指茶）
