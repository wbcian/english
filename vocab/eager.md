## Examples
> "The kids were eager to open their presents."

> 孩子們急著要拆禮物。

> "She gave an eager smile when she saw him."

> 看到他時她迫不及待地露出笑容。

> "Investors are eager for results."

> 投資人渴望看到成果。

## Usage tip

| 句型 | 例子 |
|---|---|
| `eager + to + V` | eager to learn |
| `eager + for + N` | eager for results |

**`eager beaver`** (idiom) — 形容超級熱心/積極的人（帶點調侃）
> "She's such an eager beaver — always volunteering for extra work."

## eager vs keen vs anxious

| 字 | 語感 |
|---|---|
| **keen** | 持續性興趣、長期熱衷 |
| **eager** | 立即性渴望、迫不及待 |
| **anxious** | 渴望但帶點擔憂 |

## Synonyms
enthusiastic, anxious (to do), itching (to do — 口語), raring (to go)
