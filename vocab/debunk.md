## Examples
> "This article debunks the myth that you only use 10% of your brain."
> 這篇文章戳破了「人只用大腦 10%」的迷思。

> "Scientists quickly debunked the rumor."
> 科學家很快就揭穿了那個謠言。

## Usage tip
To expose something as false using facts/evidence.
- Common objects: **a myth, a claim, a theory, a rumor**.
- Regular verb: debunk / debunked / debunked.
- Current & widely used in news, science, misinformation discussion. Sounds smart but natural.

## Synonyms
disprove, refute, expose, discredit, bust (casual: "myth-busting")
