## Examples
> "Tom and Zendaya had agreed that they would take it easy."
> Tom 和 Zendaya 講好都不要太拼。

> "Take it easy, man — you've been working all night."

> 兄弟，放輕鬆啦，你已經熬一整晚了。

> "Alright bro, take it easy."
> 好啦兄弟，保重。 (= see ya)

## Usage tip
**三種主要用法**：

| 情境 | 意思 |
|---|---|
| 勸人別太拼 | 放輕鬆、別太用力 |
| 勸人別生氣 | 冷靜、別激動 |
| 道別語 | 保重、慢走（很 casual） |

- 反義片語：**go all out**（卯足全力）
- 別跟 **easy-going** 混淆：take it easy = 動作建議；easy-going = 個性隨和
- The Eagles 1972 名曲 "Take It Easy" 把這片語推進主流文化

## Synonyms
relax, chill (out), ease up, slow down, take a breather
