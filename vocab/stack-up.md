## Examples
> "Learn one phrasal verb a day and it stacks up — by year's end you'll know 365."

> 一天學一個片語動詞，它就會積少成多——到年底你就會 365 個。

## Usage tip
= add up / accumulate（一點一點累積）。另一高頻義是 **stack up against**＝（與…）相比、比高下（How does this phone stack up against the competition?）。Thiago 在課堂上把 "it stacks up" 和 "it adds up" 連用，強調複利效果。

## Synonyms
