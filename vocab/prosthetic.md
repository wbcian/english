## Examples
> "After the accident, she learned to walk again with a prosthetic leg."
> 意外之後，她靠著一條義肢重新學會走路。

> "The movie used prosthetic makeup to age the actor by 40 years."
> 那部電影用特效化妝（義肢式假體）讓演員老了四十歲。

## Usage tip
Two main real-world contexts: **medical** (prosthetic leg/arm/hand/joint) and **film/SFX** (prosthetic makeup — fake noses, scars, wounds).
The device itself = **prosthesis** /prɑːsˈθiːsɪs/, plural **prostheses** /-siːz/.
Pronunciation: soft th /θ/ (as in "think"); stress on the middle syllable pros-THE-tic.
Topic-bound — useful but not everyday small talk.

## Synonyms
artificial limb, prosthesis
