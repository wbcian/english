## Examples
> "Her presentation was on point — clear, sharp, no fluff."
> 她的簡報精準到位——清楚、犀利、不囉嗦。

> "Your eyeliner is on point today."
> 你今天的眼線畫得超完美。

## Usage tip
Modern slang for **excellent / exactly right / flawless** — something nailed it for the situation.
- **Register = casual/modern.** Fine for chat, texting, social media, informal work banter. ❌ Avoid in formal essays / TOEIC writing.
- ⚠️ Don't confuse with **"to the point"** = 簡潔切題（不同 idiom）: "Keep it short and to the point."
- Older/literal sense = alert and ready (military / ballet "on point"), but today it almost always means flawless.
- Common subjects: outfit, makeup, performance, timing, a skill ("His comedic timing is on point").

## Synonyms
- casual: nailed it, spot-on, flawless, perfect
- formal swap: excellent, precise, exactly right
