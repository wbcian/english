## Examples
> "Do you have a hankering for coffee?"
> 你會突然很想喝咖啡嗎？

> "I've got a hankering for some late-night noodles."
> 我突然超想吃宵夜麵。

## Usage tip
`have a hankering for + 名詞` 或 `a hankering to + 動詞`。口語，常用在突然很想吃喝某樣東西的「饞」。

## Synonyms
craving, longing, yearning, itch
