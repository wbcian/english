## Examples
> "Desktop is also great for getting an at-a-glance view of everything that's happening."

> Desktop 也很適合拿到所有事情的**一眼概觀**。

> "I want an at-a-glance dashboard for our key metrics."

> 我要一個關鍵指標的**一眼概觀** dashboard。

> "You can see all your sessions at a glance."

> 你**一眼**就能看到所有的 session。

## Usage tip
**UI / Dashboard 設計圈高頻詞**。指「**不用點開、不用滑、不用細讀**，瞄一眼就懂」的設計目標。

兩種寫法：
- **連字號形容詞**：`at-a-glance view / dashboard / summary`
- **副詞片語**：`see / understand X at a glance`（沒連字號）

| 寫法 | 用法 |
|---|---|
| `at-a-glance` (adj.) | 修飾名詞 → `an at-a-glance summary` |
| `at a glance` (adv.) | 修飾動詞 → `view at a glance` |

常見搭配：
- `at-a-glance view / dashboard / overview / summary`
- `see X at a glance`
- `understand X at a glance`

### Cian 工作場合套用
- "Build an at-a-glance dashboard for the deploy status."
- "I need to see customer health at a glance."

對比近義：
- `bird's-eye view` 鳥瞰圖（更高層次）
- `overview` 概覽（更中性）
- `at-a-glance` **強調「一秒看懂」**

## Synonyms
overview, bird's-eye view, summary, quick view, snapshot
