Vocabulary Practice 詞彙練習

  • Review each vocabulary word and try to create your own sentences
  • Practice using the words in different contexts
  • Make flashcards for difficult words

Reading Comprehension 閱讀理解

  • Read the article multiple times — first for general understanding, then for details
  • Identify the main idea of each paragraph
  • Try to summarize the article in your own words

Speaking Practice 口說練習

  • Retell the story to a friend or practice partner
  • Discuss related topics and share your opinions
  • Practice pronouncing the highlighted vocabulary words
