# Hotel Check-In：國外旅館櫃檯入住實戰

<p class="lesson-subtitle">🏨 原創旅遊英語對話 · hotel front desk check-in · 3-5 min role-play</p>

## Topic & Summary

這堂課練的是你到國外旅館櫃檯 check in 的完整流程：報訂房姓名、拿出護照和信用卡、確認房型、處理「太早到」或「房型不對」、問早餐 / Wi-Fi / 退房時間，最後補一句能不能寄放行李。核心不是背單字，是讓你到櫃檯時不要腦袋當機，只剩 "Uh... booking... me..." 這種人類退化現場。

> This lesson walks through a realistic hotel check-in: giving the reservation name, showing ID and a credit card, confirming the room type, handling an early-arrival or room-type issue, asking about breakfast, Wi-Fi, check-out time, and luggage storage. The goal is not memorizing fancy travel English. The goal is staying functional when the front desk person starts speaking at normal human speed.

## 正文 · 跟讀用（櫃檯入住）

<div class="lesson-body-scroll">

> **Receptionist**
> Good afternoon. Welcome to the Harbor View Hotel. How may I help you?

> 櫃檯：午安。歡迎來到 Harbor View Hotel。請問需要什麼協助？

> **Guest**
> Hi, I'd like to check in. I have a reservation under Wu.

> 旅客：你好，我想辦理入住。我有一筆訂房，姓 Wu。

> **Receptionist**
> Sure. May I see your passport and the credit card you used for the booking?

> 櫃檯：當然。可以看一下您的護照，以及訂房時使用的信用卡嗎？

> **Guest**
> Of course. Here you go.

> 旅客：可以，給你。

> **Receptionist**
> Thank you, Mr. Wu. I found your reservation: five nights, one non-smoking queen room, checking out on Friday. Is that correct?

> 櫃檯：謝謝您，Wu 先生。我查到您的訂房：五晚，一間禁菸 queen room，星期五退房。正確嗎？

> **Guest**
> Yes, that's right. Actually, I requested a room with two beds. Would it be possible to switch?

> 旅客：對，沒錯。其實我有要求兩張床的房間。可以幫我換嗎？

> **Receptionist**
> Let me check. We do have a twin room available, but it won't be ready until 3 p.m. You're welcome to leave your bags with us while you wait.

> 櫃檯：我幫您查一下。我們有一間 twin room 可用，不過下午三點才會準備好。等待期間您可以把行李寄放在我們這裡。

> **Guest**
> That works. Could I also ask whether breakfast is included?

> 旅客：可以。也想請問早餐有包含嗎？

> **Receptionist**
> Yes, breakfast is included. It's served on the second floor from 6:30 to 10:00 a.m.

> 櫃檯：有，早餐有包含。早餐在二樓供應，時間是早上 6:30 到 10:00。

> **Guest**
> Great. And what's the Wi-Fi password?

> 旅客：太好了。那 Wi-Fi 密碼是什麼？

> **Receptionist**
> The network is HarborGuest, and the password is printed on your key card holder.

> 櫃檯：網路名稱是 HarborGuest，密碼印在您的房卡套上。

> **Guest**
> Perfect. What time is check-out?

> 旅客：太好了。請問退房時間是幾點？

> **Receptionist**
> Check-out is at 11 a.m. If you need a late check-out, please call the front desk tomorrow morning. It depends on availability.

> 櫃檯：退房時間是早上 11 點。如果您需要延後退房，明天早上可以打給櫃檯詢問。要看當天房況。

> **Guest**
> Got it. One more thing: do you need a security deposit?

> 旅客：了解。還有一件事：需要押金嗎？

> **Receptionist**
> Yes. We'll place a temporary hold of 100 dollars on your card for incidentals. It will be released after check-out if there are no extra charges.

> 櫃檯：需要。我們會在您的信用卡上暫時保留 100 美元，作為雜費押金。如果退房後沒有額外費用，這筆款項會解除。

> **Guest**
> No problem. Could you let me know when the room is ready?

> 旅客：沒問題。房間準備好時可以通知我嗎？

> **Receptionist**
> Absolutely. We'll text you as soon as it's ready. Here is your claim ticket for the luggage, and here is a map of the area.

> 櫃檯：當然。房間一準備好我們就會傳簡訊給您。這是您的行李寄放單，這是附近地圖。

> **Guest**
> Thanks. Could you point me toward the nearest convenience store?

> 旅客：謝謝。可以告訴我最近的便利商店往哪裡走嗎？

> **Receptionist**
> Sure. Turn left when you leave the hotel, walk two blocks, and you'll see it on your right.

> 櫃檯：當然。出飯店後左轉，走兩個街區，它會在您的右手邊。

> **Guest**
> Great, thank you for your help.

> 旅客：太好了，謝謝你的幫忙。

> **Receptionist**
> You're very welcome. Enjoy your stay, Mr. Wu.

> 櫃檯：不客氣。祝您住宿愉快，Wu 先生。

| non-smoking room | n.phr. | 禁菸房 | 反義：smoking room，現在很多飯店全館禁菸 |

## Sentence Anatomy

### 1. I have a reservation under Wu.

`under + 姓氏` 在櫃檯英文裡超自然。不要直譯成 "My booking name is Wu"。能懂，但味道像機器人穿西裝。

| chunk | function | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| I have | 主詞 + 動詞 | 我有 |
| a reservation | 受詞 | 一筆訂房 |
| under Wu | 名字掛在哪個姓氏底下 | 姓 Wu |

**替換練習**

- I have a reservation under Chen.
- I have a reservation under Lin.
- The booking should be under Wu.

### 2. Would it be possible to switch?

這句是旅行英文神句。比 "Can I switch?" 更客氣，適合拿來問換房、提早入住、延後退房、換座位。旅館櫃檯聽到會覺得：這位旅客至少不是來開戰的，謝天謝地。

| pattern | example |
|---|---|
| Would it be possible to + V? | Would it be possible to switch rooms? |
| Would it be possible to + V? | Would it be possible to check in early? |
| Would it be possible to + V? | Would it be possible to leave my bags here? |

## Connected Speech / Pronunciation

| 原句 | 自然口語會像 | 重點 |
|---|---|---|
| I'd like to check in. | I'd like tuh check in. | `to` 弱化成 /tə/ |
| under Wu | under Wu | `under` 的 r 要接到 Wu 前面，別斷太開 |
| Is that correct? | Iz that correct? | `is` 的 /z/ 連到 that |
| It depends on availability. | It depends on availability. | `availability` 重音在 `bil`：a-vail-a-BIL-i-ty |
| temporary hold | temporary hold | `temporary` 美式常念成三拍：TEM-puh-rer-y |

## Cultural Notes

- **Credit card for incidentals**：很多飯店不是不信任你，是流程需要。這通常是 temporary hold，不一定是正式扣款。
- **Early check-in**：提早到時不要直接說 "I need my room now." 改成 "Would it be possible to check in early?" 比較像文明社會的人類。
- **Breakfast included?**：這句一定要問清楚。國外飯店早餐常常貴到你會懷疑那盤 scrambled eggs 是不是有博士學位。
- **Front desk vs reception**：美式常說 front desk，英式 / 國際飯店也常看到 reception。兩個都懂。

## Role-Play Drill

把下面空格換成自己的資訊，唸三輪：第一輪慢、第二輪正常、第三輪假裝後面有十個人在排隊。

> **Guest**
> Hi, I'd like to check in. I have a reservation under ____.

> 旅客：你好，我想辦理入住。我有一筆姓 ____ 的訂房。

> **Receptionist**
> May I see your passport and a credit card, please?

> 櫃檯：可以看一下您的護照和信用卡嗎？

> **Guest**
> Of course. Here you go. Also, would it be possible to ____?

> 旅客：可以，給你。另外，可以幫我 ____ 嗎？

> **Receptionist**
> Let me check. It depends on availability.

> 櫃檯：我幫您查一下。這要看房況。

可替換動作：

| request | 中文 |
|---|---|
| check in early | 提早入住 |
| switch to a twin room | 換成雙床房 |
| get a room on a higher floor | 換到高樓層房間 |
| leave my bags here | 把行李寄放在這裡 |
| have a late check-out | 延後退房 |

## Study Tips / Takeaways

- **先背流程，不要先背單字**：check in → ID/card → room details → deposit → breakfast/Wi-Fi/check-out → luggage。流程順了，單字自然有地方放。
- **`Would it be possible to...?` 是安全牌**：你需要麻煩櫃檯時，用這句開頭，禮貌值直接 +50。
- **`under + last name` 要練到反射**：I have a reservation under Wu. 這句比 "My name is..." 更像真的在辦入住。
- **聽到 `incidentals` 不要慌**：通常是押金/預授權，代表 minibar、客房服務、損壞費用等額外費用。
- **最後加一個實用問題**：nearest convenience store / luggage storage / late check-out，這種問題比課本句更接近真旅行。
