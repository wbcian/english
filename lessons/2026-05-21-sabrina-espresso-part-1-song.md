> Example（02:14）：「那我猜這代表我們結束了。——對啊，我猜是吧。算了，算了。」（下一句 "Move it up…" 為歌詞，保留英文）

> **Ethan (02:24)**
> To **switch something up** means to do something differently from how you've been doing it. Think about how Beyoncé constantly switches it up — one album she's doing country, the next she's doing disco. She never lets things get boring. It's also a **play on words** with the Nintendo Switch console.
>
> Finally, this song is great for understanding fast English because Sabrina uses a ton of **connected speech** — how we natives cut, reduce, and link our words. Listen to: "Say you can't sleep, baby, I know. That's that me espresso." You'd expect "that's that" with full T's, right? But in "that's" the second T disappears altogether — [thas]. This happens with **TS combinations**: *What's her deal?* → [wus-sur deal]; *It's about time* → [iss-suh-bouh time]; *Let's have a coffee* → [les-sa-vuh coffee]. And in "that" the second T is cut off at the back of the throat — a **glottal T**, like a kid saying "Uh-oh." So she sings [thass-tha'-me espresso]. Try singing it with Sabrina!

> Ethan（02:24）：switch something up 指把一件事做得跟以往不一樣。想想 Beyoncé 一直在 switch it up——這張專輯做鄉村，下一張做 disco，從不讓人覺得無聊。它也跟任天堂 Nintendo Switch 形成 play on words（雙關）。
>
> 最後，這首歌很適合練「聽快速英文」，因為 Sabrina 用了大量 connected speech（連音）——母語者怎麼切音、弱化、連讀。（示範句為歌詞，保留英文）你以為 "that's that" 的 T 都會清楚發出來對吧？但 "that's" 的第二個 T 整個消失——[thas]。這發生在 TS combinations：What's her deal? → [wus-sur deal]；It's about time → [iss-suh-bouh time]；Let's have a coffee → [les-sa-vuh coffee]。而 "that" 的第二個 T 在喉嚨後方被切掉——glottal T，像小孩說 "Uh-oh"。跟著 Sabrina 一起唱唱看！

## 核心主題：Connected Speech 入門

母語者講快時會 **cut（吃音）/ reduce（弱化）/ link（連音）**。Part 1 先抓兩條規則：

### 🎯 Rule 1：TS → S（TS 組合，T 消失，S 拉長並連到下個字）

| word | 唸出來 | 拆解 |
|---|---|---|
| that's that | [thass-tha'] | 第二個 T 消失，S 拉長並連到下個字 |
| What's her deal? | [wus-sur deal] | what's → wus，S 連到 her |
| It's about time | [iss-suh-bouh time] | it's → iss，about 的 T 也軟化 |
| Let's have a coffee | [les-sa-vuh coffee] | let's → les，have a 連成 "sa-vuh" |

### 🎯 Rule 2：Glottal T（字尾 T 在喉嚨後方卡掉、不真的發音）

像 "uh-oh" 中間那一頓。例：that → [tha']、important → [impor'ant]。

> **下一條規則（tap T → 母音間 T 軟化成 D，像 *butter* → [bu-der]）會在 [Part 2](2026-05-23-sabrina-espresso-part-2-discourse.md) 介紹。**

## Useful Phrases & Patterns

- **have a hankering for ~** 突然很想要／很饞～
  > I've got a hankering for late-night ramen. 我突然超想吃宵夜拉麵。
- **a play on words** 雙關語
  > "Switch it up" is a play on words with the Nintendo Switch.
- **switch from A to B / switch it up** 改變做法、換花樣

## Study Tips / Takeaways

- **`I guess so` 的語氣**：不是熱情的「好啊」，是「嗯…大概吧」。回答 "Was it good?" 用它＝你其實覺得普普。想表達肯定別用這個。
- **`cup of Joe`** 跟咖啡店點餐超好用，記下來能瞬間變道地。
- **連音是聽力卡關的真兇**：你聽不懂母語者，常常不是單字不會，而是字被連在一起、T 被吃掉了。多跟讀歌詞是最快的破解法。
- **雙關（play on words）**：英文歌名／廣告超愛玩，`switch it up` ↔ Nintendo Switch 就是一例。
