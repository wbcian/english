# Forget Me Not — 妥瑞症女孩的漂泊與救贖

<p class="lesson-subtitle">🎧 English Digest 實用空中美語 · Unit 15-1 文學單元（兩天單元 Day 1）· Tom & Stephanie 英解 + Patrick 老師中解 · ~32 min</p>

## Topic & Summary

空中美語文學單元選了 Ellie Terry 的處女作小說 **Forget Me Not**（中譯《月亮都是一樣的》）。主角 **Calliope Snow（暱稱 Callie）** 是個有妥瑞症（Tourette syndrome）的女孩：爸爸車禍過世後，媽媽情緒不穩、不斷換男友，每次分手就搬家，Callie 已經搬到第十次、從沒在任何地方「扎過根」。母親和醫師都要她把病藏起來，但妥瑞症藏不住，換來的是同學的注視、竊竊私語與霸凌。這集把整本書的劇情分兩幕摘要朗讀完，Day 2 才深入談作者。對 Cian 最有料的地方：這是一篇**乾淨的敘事型導讀**，字彙密度高又都是 TOEIC 會考的字（relocation / conceal / physician / prominent / disclose），而且 Patrick 老師直接示範了怎麼拆**分詞構句**——正中你的句構弱點。

*The English Digest literature unit picks Ellie Terry's debut novel* **Forget Me Not**. *The main character,* **Calliope Snow (Callie)**, *has Tourette syndrome. After her father dies, her unstable mother hops from boyfriend to boyfriend, and every breakup means another move — Callie is now on her tenth relocation and has never felt rooted anywhere. Both her mother and physician urge her to hide her condition, but the tics can't be concealed, and stares, whispers, and bullying follow her. This episode summarizes the whole plot in two acts; Day 2 will go deeper on the author. Best part for you: it's a clean narrative read, packed with high-value TOEIC words, and the Chinese teacher literally walks through a reduced participial clause — right at your weak spot.*

## Close Reading · 課文全文（兩幕，可朗讀 🔊）

> **Act 1 — The rootless girl**
> Forget Me Not brings Tourette syndrome to light. Calliope Snow, known as Callie, has never stayed anywhere long enough to feel rooted.

> 第一幕——扎不了根的女孩。《Forget Me Not》讓妥瑞症被世人看見。凱莉歐碧・史諾，大家叫她 Callie，從來沒有在任何地方待得夠久、久到能有歸屬感。

> After her father dies in a car accident, her mother becomes unstable and dependent on boyfriends. Every breakup means another move. Now on her 10th relocation, Callie desperately prays for something different: friends and a place that finally feels like home.

> 爸爸車禍過世後，媽媽變得情緒不穩、離不開男朋友。每一次分手就代表又要搬家。如今搬到第十次，Callie 拚命祈求能有點不一樣的東西：朋友，還有一個終於有家的感覺的地方。

> However, Callie carries a secret that refuses to stay hidden. She has Tourette syndrome, a neurological condition triggering uncontrollable sounds and movements.

> 然而，Callie 藏著一個藏不住的秘密。她有妥瑞症——一種會引發不自主的聲音與動作的神經性疾病。

> Both her mother and physician urge her to hide it. On her first day, she wears baggy clothes and hopes classmates notice her outfits, not her tics.

> 媽媽和醫師都力勸她把病藏起來。開學第一天，她穿著寬鬆的衣服，希望同學注意到的是她的穿搭，而不是她的抽動。

> They notice anyway, and stares, whispers, and bullying follow her everywhere.

> 但同學還是發現了，於是注視、竊竊私語和霸凌如影隨形。

> **Act 2 — The one exception**
> The only exception is Jin Sung, her Chinese-American neighbor and the school's prominent student body president. Drawn to Callie from the day she moves in, Jin Sung dreads losing his reputation.

> 第二幕——唯一的例外。唯一的例外是靜松，她的華裔美籍鄰居，也是學校裡備受矚目的學生會會長。從她搬進來那天起就被她吸引，靜松卻很怕壞了自己的名聲。

> He talks with her after school, but avoids her in public. Still, his sincerity builds trust and a genuine friendship blossoms.

> 他放學後會跟她聊天，但在公開場合避著她。儘管如此，他的真誠慢慢建立起信任，一段真摯的友誼就此萌芽。

> When his friends mock Callie again, Jin Sung erupts, punches them, and ends up being suspended.

> 當他的朋友再次嘲弄 Callie，靜松爆發了，揍了他們，最後被停學。

> Then, Callie's world shifts without warning. Her mother impulsively marries a new boyfriend and announces a move to Las Vegas, Nevada.

> 接著，Callie 的世界毫無預警地翻轉。她媽媽衝動地嫁給新男友，宣布要搬到內華達州的拉斯維加斯。

> Before she departs, Callie shares one last walk with Jin Sung, and he kisses her farewell.

> 離開前，Callie 和靜松最後一起散了一次步，他給了她一個告別之吻。

> On the drive, Callie confronts her mother about the pain of constant moving and concealing her condition. Her mother apologizes, and they begin communicating more openly.

> 在車上，Callie 跟媽媽攤牌，說出不斷搬家、又得隱瞞病情有多痛苦。媽媽道了歉，兩人開始更坦誠地溝通。

> At her new school, Callie introduces herself honestly and discloses her Tourette syndrome. Surprisingly, her classmates respond with warmth and acceptance. Callie and Jin Sung stay in touch, and she's already planning a summer visit.

> 在新學校，Callie 誠實地自我介紹，主動坦白自己有妥瑞症。出乎意料地，同學們以溫暖和接納回應。Callie 和靜松保持聯絡，她已經在計畫暑假回去探望了。

## Reading Comprehension · 模擬 TOEIC Part 7

先自己作答，再點開答案。

**Q1 · Main idea** — What is the main reason Callie has "never stayed anywhere long enough to feel rooted"?

- (A) She is in the U.S. military and moves between bases
- (B) Her mother keeps moving after each breakup with a boyfriend
- (C) She keeps getting suspended from school
- (D) Her Tourette syndrome forces her to change schools

<details><summary>答案 + 解析</summary>

**B**。原文：*"her mother becomes unstable and dependent on boyfriends. Every breakup means another move."* (A) 是主持人拿來**類比**的例子（military brats），不是 Callie 本人；(C)(D) 沒有原文支撐。

</details>

**Q2 · Vocab in context** — In "Both her mother and **physician** urge her to hide it," *physician* most nearly means:

- (A) A teacher
- (B) A lawyer
- (C) A doctor
- (D) A therapist

<details><summary>答案 + 解析</summary>

**C**。physician 就是 doctor 的正式版。主持人原話：*"Physician is another word for doctor. They're both correct."*

</details>

**Q3 · Inference** — Why does Jin Sung talk to Callie after school but avoid her in public?

- (A) He is not actually interested in her
- (B) He is worried about damaging his reputation as student body president
- (C) His parents forbid him from seeing her
- (D) He is also being bullied

<details><summary>答案 + 解析</summary>

**B**。原文：*"Jin Sung dreads losing his reputation… avoids her in public."* 他是備受矚目的學生會長，怕跟被霸凌的女孩走太近會影響形象。

</details>

**Q4 · Detail** — What finally happens when Callie **discloses** her Tourette syndrome at her new school?

- (A) She gets bullied even worse
- (B) The teachers ask her to hide it again
- (C) Her classmates respond with warmth and acceptance
- (D) She has to relocate one more time

<details><summary>答案 + 解析</summary>

**C**。原文：*"she introduces herself honestly and discloses her Tourette syndrome. Surprisingly, her classmates respond with warmth and acceptance."* 主動坦白反而換來接納——正好推翻「藏起來」的建議。

</details>

**Q5 · Application（自由作答）** — 用英文寫 3-4 句：如果你是 Callie，到新學校時你會選擇 `conceal` 還是 `disclose` 你的狀況？為什麼？句子裡至少用到 `conceal`、`disclose`、`reputation` 其中兩個。寫完貼回來，Coach Max 幫你抓 native 度。

## Sentence Anatomy · 拆 2 個漂亮句子

補你「中翻英直譯」的弱點——看英文怎麼把資訊壓進一個句子還不亂。這兩句 Patrick 老師在節目裡也親自拆過。

### 句 1：分詞構句（reduced participial clause）

**Drawn to Callie from the day she moves in, Jin Sung dreads losing his reputation.**

骨架：`(Being) + p.p. …, 主詞 + 動詞 …`

- 完整版是 `Jin Sung is drawn to Callie…, and he dreads…`。因為兩句**主詞相同（Jin Sung）**，前半就砍掉主詞和 be 動詞，只留過去分詞 `Drawn to…` 當開頭——這就是**分詞構句**。
- `drawn to sb`＝被某人吸引（draw 的被動）。開頭用分詞 = native 敘事的「輕量開場」，比 `Because he was drawn to her,` 乾淨。
- ⚠️ **你的老陷阱（懸垂分詞）**：開頭分詞的隱藏主詞，一定要等於主句主詞。這裡 `Drawn to Callie` 的人是 Jin Sung，主句主詞也是 Jin Sung ✅。若寫成 `Drawn to Callie, her reputation…` 就爛掉了（被吸引的不是 reputation）。
- 可套句：`Raised in three different cities, she makes friends fast.`（＝Because she was raised…）

### 句 2：擬人 + 關係子句

**Callie carries a secret that refuses to stay hidden.**

- `a secret that refuses to stay hidden`＝一個「拒絕躲藏」的秘密。秘密沒有意志，作者卻用 `refuses`（拒絕）**擬人化**，畫面立刻活起來——像個不受控的調皮小孩。Patrick 老師特別稱讚這個動詞用得好。
- `stay + 形容詞`＝維持某狀態（stay hidden／stay calm／stay put）。
- 可套句：`He has a talent that refuses to stay quiet.`（一個藏不住的才華）

## Cultural Notes · 順帶懂的文化點

- **Forget-me-not（勿忘我）**：一種藍色小花，花語就是「別忘記我」。作者拿它當書名一語雙關——既是花，也是在說「別忘了去理解、記得妥瑞症患者」。主持人說 `bring sth to light`＝讓某事**被看見／攤在陽光下**（喚起大眾意識）。
- **military brat（軍人子女）**：在美國指「爸媽是軍人、從小跟著到處搬家」的孩子。⚠️ brat 單獨用是「屁孩」有貶意，但 `military brat` 是**中性甚至自稱**的稱呼，這群人通常超會交朋友、適應力強。
- **student body president（學生會會長）**：student body＝全校學生的總稱。美國高中每年**選舉**學生會長，候選人會開競選政見（多裝點心販賣機、多放假之類），選上代表人氣高、`prominent`（顯眼、有份量）。
- **physician 的語感**：跟 doctor 同義但更正式書面。主持人 Tom 開玩笑說 physician 聽起來「比較高級」（他直接夾了一句中文 gaoji），但其實日常兩個都通。
- **debut /deɪˈbjuː/**：法文借詞，**字尾 t 不發音**。`debut novel`＝處女作小說、`debut album`＝出道專輯、`debut recital`＝首場獨奏會——都是「第一次公開亮相」。
- **Las Vegas, Nevada**：內華達州拉斯維加斯，美國的「賭城」（gambling capital）。媽媽衝動再婚後宣布搬去這裡。

## Study Tips · 這篇帶走 3 件事

1. **conceal ↔ disclose 是一對反義**：這篇整個張力就是「藏（conceal）vs 坦白（disclose）」。背單字時成對記最省力，而且兩個都是 TOEIC 閱讀高頻——`conceal information` / `disclose information` 直接可用。
2. **開頭分詞 = 敘事升級鍵**：想讓句子不要每句都 `He was…, and he…`，把其中一句改成開頭分詞（`Drawn to…`, `Raised in…`, `Known as…`）。但**一定檢查隱藏主詞**對不對得上主句——這是你上次面試稿踩過的雷。
3. **break up（動）vs breakup（名）**：分開兩個字是動詞片語（`break up with him`），連成一個字是名詞（`a messy breakup`）。同源不同詞性靠「空格」區分，這類 TOEIC 詞性題很愛考。
